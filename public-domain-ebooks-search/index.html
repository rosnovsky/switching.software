WHICH FILE FORMAT?

If you’re unsure which eBook format to download, generally “ePub” works on everything except Kindle, while “Mobi” works on Kindle.

“PDF” and “HTML” are best viewed on computers. “TXT” works on everything but may not look as good.

 

HELP! WHERE ARE THE eBOOKS ON WIKISOURCE?

To download eBooks from Wikisource, click on the download link at the side of the page as shown on this screenshot (or on this screenshot for the mobile version).

To download an entire eBook instead of just one chapter, go to the contents page for that book and click on the download link there.
