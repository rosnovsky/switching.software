Ethical alternatives to Google Forms

LimeSurvey

Free open source survey software, it can be used on the LimeSurvey.org site, or you can install it on your own server if you have one.

The self-hosted version is completely free of charge, while the version hosted by LimeSurvey comes in free and paid versions.

WEBSITE (HOSTED & SELF-HOSTED) – LimeSurvey.org

 

Webform

Free open source form software, available to either self-host or host on Webform.com. The version hosted on Webform.com comes in free and paid versions.

WEBSITE (HOSTED) – Webform.com
